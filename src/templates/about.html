Champions of inspired luxury vacation rentals

Industry research and his own travel experience led Luxeberry’s founder, Max Sheppard, to make two stark conclusions: demand outstrips supply for high-end quality short-term vacation rentals and existing online platforms fail to capitalize on this lucrative market in Canada.

Thus the Luxeberry Group was born with the mission to pair the best vacation rentals with low-risk guests that appreciate the finer details.

A Passion for Real Estate

From starting a thriving retail business while in high school to over a decade operating a prominent digital marketing and development firm to creating, expanding, and selling a widely successful home improvement website, Max has over three decades of experience in the business world.

Throughout his career, Max has always kept a keen eye on the real estate sector, purchasing and managing conventional investment properties. At the same time, his curiosity for the short-term rental space evolved into an obsession. After selling his business, Max focused his energy on developing tools to help ensure complete guest satisfaction while increasing returns for property owners.

But to acquire enough real-world experience to manage short term vacation rentals on behalf of other property owners, Max knew he had the give the business a go firsthand. Upon purchasing and renovating investment properties in Grand Bend and Tobermory, Max achieved perfect five-star ratings from all guests, even the most discerning ones, while generated higher returns compared to most conventional rental properties.
