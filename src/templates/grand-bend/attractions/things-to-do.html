Exploring Grand Bend and Lampton Shores

Stretching along the shores of Lake Huron, Ontario's Grand Bend has garnered a well-earned rep as the best beach resort in Canada. Scores of sun-worshippers descend on its lively Main Beach each summer, where epic sunsets paint vivid hues of red and gold. Factor in exceptional water quality and a dedicated lifeguard service, and you've got a premium lakeside beach resort that's second to none.
